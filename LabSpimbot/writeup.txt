Strategy:
	The goal of this project is to wrtie a bot which helps grow the mass of the allegiance planet to be larger than that of opponent's planet by attracting dust particles. In order to attract dust particles, we scan through the zuniverse and find the sector with the most dust particles. After we have located the dust, we go to this sector and grab the particles bring to our planet. When we run out of energy, out bot just stop and solve puzzle faithfully. And we got energy again!!! For the interference part, we will turn back to avoid our rival.
